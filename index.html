Facility/Cost

Technicolabs Softwares offers top-notch web development services to enhance your online presence. Our skilled team creates visually appealing, user-friendly websites with the latest technologies. From custom designs to e-commerce solutions, we ensure your digital success.

Web Development

The cost of developing a website in Pakistan varies widely. A basic informational site can cost PKR 30,000 to PKR 50,000, while a standard business site ranges from PKR 50,000 to PKR 150,000. E-commerce sites, with features like shopping carts, range from PKR 100,000 to PKR 300,000+. Custom web applications cost between PKR 200,000 to PKR 500,000+, and premium enterprise-level sites can exceed PKR 500,000. These costs depend on project complexity, features, and additional services like SEO and maintenance.

UI/UX

The cost of UI/UX design in Pakistan varies based on the project's complexity and the designer's expertise. Basic UI/UX design for a small app or website can cost between PKR 30,000 to PKR 70,000. For more comprehensive projects, such as a full website or complex app, costs range from PKR 70,000 to PKR 200,000. High-end, custom UI/UX design for large-scale projects or enterprise applications can exceed PKR 200,000, reaching up to PKR 500,000 or more. These prices include user research, wireframing, prototyping, and final design implementation.

Mobile App Dev

The cost of mobile app development in Pakistan varies widely based on the app's complexity and features. Simple apps with basic functionality can cost between PKR 50,000 to PKR 150,000. Medium complexity apps, with features like user authentication and integration with APIs, range from PKR 150,000 to PKR 400,000. High-end, complex apps, including advanced features such as real-time updates, payment gateways, and custom animations, can cost between PKR 400,000 to PKR 1,000,000 or more. These estimates cover the entire development process, from initial design to final deployment and testing.

Digital Marketing

The cost of digital marketing services in Pakistan varies depending on the scope and strategy. Basic digital marketing services, including social media management and basic SEO, typically range from PKR 20,000 to PKR 50,000 per month. More comprehensive packages, including content creation, advanced SEO, and PPC campaigns, can cost between PKR 50,000 to PKR 150,000 per month. High-end digital marketing strategies, tailored for large businesses with extensive campaigns and detailed analytics, can exceed PKR 150,000 per month, reaching up to PKR 500,000 or more. These costs cover planning, execution, and monitoring of digital marketing activities.
